Xenopulse
A fast-paced, 3D action combat prototype built in Godot 4. Xenopulse focuses on visceral, physics-driven melee combat where momentum, timing, and precision matter. Inspired by the directional combat systems of Mount & Blade, players must manage their swings, string together combos, and fight off endless waves of dynamically scaling enemies.

✨ Features
⚔️ Directional Melee Combat
Mount & Blade Style Swings: Hold the attack button, drag the mouse to choose your swing direction (Left, Right, Overhead, or Thrust), and release to strike.
Momentum-Based Damage: Damage scales with your windup time, and significantly increases based on your player's physical velocity. Run faster to hit harder!
Physics Knockback: Slashing enemies applies massive kinetic force, sending them flying backward with realistic gravity and friction.
👹 Dynamic Enemy Spawning & Scaling
Procedural Scaling: The AI spawner constantly generates enemies with randomized health pools. Their physical size is seamlessly tied to their maximum health—the tankier the enemy, the more massive they are!
Adaptive Knockback Tolerance: Large, high-health enemies naturally resist physical knockback, forcing you to use heavier momentum swings to push them away.
Relentless AI: Enemies utilize physics-based follow AI to track and corner the player around the arena.
🔥 Combo System & Dynamic Atmosphere
Kill Streaks: String together rapid kills to build your Combo Meter. If you go 1 second without securing a kill, the combo breaks.
Reactive Lighting: The world reacts to your rampage. As your combo tier increases, the environment's sunlight dynamically shifts from peaceful daylight to a deep, blood-red hue.
🏃 Advanced Player Mobility
Fluid Movement: Momentum-driven character controller with sprinting, jumping, and mid-air directional control.
Burst Dashing: Use a dedicated dash mechanic to quickly close the gap for a velocity-boosted strike, or to dodge out of a swarm of enemies.
Smooth Camera: Fully unlocked, responsive 3rd-person spring-arm camera that allows you to assess the battlefield while charging attacks.
🛠️ Built With
Engine: Godot 4 (GDScript)
Physics: Godot 3D Physics Engine (CharacterBody3D, RayCasts, ShapeQueries)
